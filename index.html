FAQ

Questions, answered

What exactly is press release distribution?

It's the process of getting your news written up and published across a network of news sites, finance portals, and industry outlets — so your announcement lives on real, public, indexable pages instead of just your own website or social feed.

How fast will my release go live?

Most releases are live within 24–48 hours after you approve the final draft. Individual outlets in the network may pick it up on their own schedules after that.

Will this actually help with AI tools like ChatGPT or Perplexity?

AI answer engines generate responses based on content that's already published and indexed across the web. They can't cite what doesn't exist online. By putting your announcement on credible, crawlable outlets, you increase the chance that your facts, quotes, and brand name are part of what these tools pull from — we don't control any AI model's output, but we control whether your story is in the source material.

Do you write the press release for me?

You can send us a finished draft, or rough notes and we'll shape it into a proper release. Every submission goes through editorial review regardless.

Is placement on every outlet guaranteed?

Distribution to our network is guaranteed. Because individual outlets curate their own sites, exact pickup varies by outlet and news cycle — your coverage report will show you precisely where your release published.

What kind of announcements work best?

Product launches, funding rounds, partnerships, leadership hires, milestones, and company news all work well. If you're not sure your news is ready, tell us what you've got and we'll advise.
